V16.36_INTERMITTENT_TEST_REMARKS

Scope:
- app.js was not modified.
- 3-STEP finalized flow was not modified.
- Intermittent Test was implemented in ui.js / index.html / style.css only.

Intermittent Test logic:
- Load KP = body weight × 7.5%, rounded to 0.1 KP.
- 10 rounds total.
- Each round = 5 sec WORK + 20 sec REST.
- RPM samples are collected only during the 5 sec WORK phase.
- Rest phase is not included in RPM average or watt calculation.
- Invalid RPM samples below 20 rpm are ignored.
- Avg Watt per round = KP × Avg RPM × 0.98.
- Result chart: Y-axis = Average Power per round, X-axis = Round 1 to 10.

Safety/compatibility:
- Existing FREE RIDE / ERG / 3-STEP pages and app.js BLE/control functions are preserved.
- Intermittent control tries to use the existing global sendKP() from app.js when available.
