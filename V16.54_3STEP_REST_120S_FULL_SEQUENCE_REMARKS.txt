3-STEP sequence:

2. START sends the STEP 1 KP command and runs WORK for 10 seconds.
3. After STEP 1, the output KP is cleared for REST.
4. During REST, the next STEP KP is calculated from the detected max RPM.
5. REST lasts 120 seconds.
6. STEP 2 sends the calculated KP and runs WORK for 10 seconds.
7. After STEP 2, the output KP is cleared for REST.
8. During REST, STEP 3 KP is calculated from the detected max RPM.
9. REST lasts 120 seconds.
10. STEP 3 sends the calculated KP and runs WORK for 10 seconds.
11. After STEP 3, the test enters the result page.
